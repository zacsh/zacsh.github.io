Instruction & Adjudication

I am a Head Instructor at the United Spirit Association Drum Major program, where I teach student drum majors across California about parade, field conducting, and leadership techniques. I also teach private lessons: notably, one of my students won the 2026 California State Drum Major Championship.

I am a parade adjudicator with the Peacocks American Drum Major Competitions Circuit.

Performance

Recently, I performed in the FIFA 2026 World Cup Opening Ceremonies in Los Angeles, California as drum major. You can see my performance from 0:00 – 3:09 here.

In 2025, I was a drum major for the Royal Edinburgh Military Tattoo Pipes and Drums in Edinburgh, Scotland.

I choreographed the flourishing drum major display for the 2024 and 2025 Estes Park International Military Tattoo in Colorado.

I won the Western United States Juvenile Drum Major Championships in 2022 and 2023; the California Drum Major State Championship in 2023 and 2024; and the Northern California Drum Major Championship in 2022, 2023, and 2024.
